About Infernal Festival

Set against the grand, historic backdrop of Scorrier House in Cornwall, Infernal Festival brings the heart of the metal and alternative scene to life each summer.

For three electrifying nights, we unite legendary headliners with the fiercest new bands shaking up the scene.

Infernal Festival is a full-throttle celebration of heavy music, raw energy, and unforgettable moments.
